Development Process

1

Risk Assessment & Analysis

Identifies security gaps and evaluates vulnerabilities in systems, networks, and applications. Helps organizations understand potential cyber risks and develop a risk management strategy.

2

Prevention & Protection/h3>

Implements firewalls, encryption, access controls, and endpoint security to prevent unauthorized access. Strengthens security posture with multi-factor authentication (MFA) and zero-trust architecture.

3

Threat Detection & Monitoring

Continuously monitors IT infrastructure using AI-powered threat intelligence and SIEM tools. Detects abnormal behavior, phishing attempts, and malware infections before they escalate.

4

Incident Response & Mitigation

Establishes a structured Incident Response Plan (IRP) for handling cyberattacks. Focuses on early detection, containment, and neutralization of threats to minimize damage.

5

Recovery & Business Continuity

Restores affected systems and data through secure backup and disaster recovery solutions. Enhances security measures based on forensic analysis of security incidents.

6

Compliance & Governance

Ensures businesses follow industry regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001, and PCI-DSS. Conducts security audits, employee training, and policy updates to maintain compliance.
